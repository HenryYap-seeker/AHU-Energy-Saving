According to the ASHRAE standard 62.1 User's Manual, HVAC equipment accounts more than 60% of a building's energy usage and air-handling units (AHU) is the main sources of energy use (more than 40%).

Efficient air-handling units and air distribution systems can save significant amounts of energy, reduce operating costs and comply with widely used energy standards and building codes.

This web application guide will focus on Air-Handling Units filter section design considerations that improve performance and reduce energy consumption.
