In the context of increasing energy prices and the imperative of reducing CO2 emissions, the energy consumption related to air filters is in the focus of attention.

The energy consumption of air filters is determined by:

  • The volume flow rate
  • The fan efficiency
  • The operation time
  • The average pressure drop

Therefore, by equipping energy-efficient filters with high filtration efficiency into the AHUs, costs and CO2 footprint can be reduce significantly.
